Rondo School of Discovery

Philistine Rondo School of Discovery is a new Elementary Magnet School in Eastvale. This 4-track year-round school will feature a Mandarin Immersion program on D track, and a STEAM instructional framework on all tracks. STEAM is a project based instructional framework that is all day every day, not a period. STEAM is an educational approach to learning that uses science, technology, engineering, the arts and math as access points for guiding student inquiry, dialogue, and critical thinking. Students will be prepared for college and career through the Leader in Me process as we teach students to lead themselves using the 7 Habits of Highly effective People and the 4 Disciplines of Execution.
